How It Works

Remote Production, Zero Compromise

Hire a full Italian production crew without the travel costs. You stay in control — we deliver the footage.

1

Brief & Plan

Share your creative brief, shot list, and schedule. We scout locations, handle permits, and prepare the technical setup tailored to your needs.

2

Shoot & Stream

On shoot day, watch everything live via secure stream. Direct our crew in real-time. Proxies upload to your cloud as we shoot — no waiting.

Deliver

Full-resolution masters delivered to your cloud or hard drive. Organized, logged, and ready for your post-production pipeline.
